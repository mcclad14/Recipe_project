Simple Baked chicken breast

Deliciously simple baked chicken breast recipe. Perfect for cooking a family dinner last minute, or prepping meals fora busy week on the go!

For some added flavor try this recipe with some cilantro lime rice to give the dish a new twist

Ingredients

  • boneless chicken breasr
  • olive oil
  • corse sea Salt
  • creole seasoning
  • water

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. rub chicken with olive oil and sprinkle salt and creole seasoning across both sides
  3. bake in pre heated oven for 25 minutes flipping halfway through
  4. Enjoy delecious baked chicken
