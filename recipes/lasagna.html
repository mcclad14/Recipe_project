Sausage and cheese Lasgna

Traditional American Lasagna dish. melted cheese combined with lasgna pasta and sausage make for an extrodinary dinner.

Have this meal same day in as little as 2 hours or let the dish settle overnight for extrodinary flavor.

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground italian sausage
  • maranara sauce
  • Salt
  • garlic

Steps

  1. pre boil Lasgna noodles
  2. brown ground sausage until cooked all the way through
  3. mix sausage into marinara sauce and bring to a boil then return to simmer
  4. Layer cooked noodles with pasta sauce and top with cheese
  5. bake at 400 degrees F for 30 min
  6. For extra flavor let sit overnight and heat to serve next day
