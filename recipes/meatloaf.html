Meat Loaf

Delicious Meatloaf

Today we will be trying one of my favorite recipes My mother would make me as a child. I always loved the mix of flavors I would get when tastig this recipe so I decided to give it try myself.

This recipe while not quite the same as my moms has a few extra ingredients for flavor to take the recipe to the next generation!

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • eggs
  • worcestershire sauce
  • italian seasoned bread crumbs

steps

  1. pre heat ovento 375 degrees
  2. mix ground beef breadcrumbs eggs and seasoning mic it into a ball and place in loaf dish.
  3. bake 45 minutes in the pre-heated oven
  4. Thats it you're all done enjoy!
